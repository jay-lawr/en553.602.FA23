How to do the pitch
- We need to convince that our method will work and be good
- Introduction
    - short and sweet
    - Who are you and why are you here
    - The impression you want them to walk away with
- Problem
    - understanding the problem 
- Solution
    - Identify a concise and clear Solution
- Market size and opportunity
    - How big is this opportunity
- Product
    - What is your Product
    - What is great about it 
- Traction
    - Show metrics and validation
    - Mention assumptions used to validate
- Team
    - Talk about the team 
- Your ask
    - What are you looking for
- Competition
- Financials
- One more thing
